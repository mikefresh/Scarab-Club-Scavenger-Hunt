Fountain

Fountain is a readymade sculpture by Marcel Duchamp in 1917,consisting of a porcelain urinal signed “R. Mutt”. The work is regarded by art historians and theorists of the avant-garde as a major landmark in 20th century art. Sixteen replicas were commissioned from Duchamp in the 1950s and 1960s and made to his approval..

Scrab club

The Scarab Club is an artists’ club, gallery, and studio in the Cultural Center Historic District of Detroit, Michigan, located at 217 Farnsworth Street, near the Detroit Institute of Arts and the Detroit Science Center..
