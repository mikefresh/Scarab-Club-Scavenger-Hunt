Scarab Club

HOW-TO-PLAY

Start the game, if you have an account, you can login to access your game history.

Once you’ve started the game, you’ll begin receiving clues, these clues are tied to various locations throughout the Cultural Center of Detroit. Guess the clues and follow the map to unlock hidden augmented reality objects.

If you get stuck on a clue, don’t worry, there are helpful hints to get you where you’re going.

Once you’ve completed a clue the next clue will become available until you’ve completed the scavenger hunt. Upon completion you’ll receive a badge that you can collect.

Each badge is worth points, you can see you badges as well as the leaderboard of other players. Keep playing to gain more points and more badges!
