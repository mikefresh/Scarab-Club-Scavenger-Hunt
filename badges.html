General Badge Info


DIA
This badge celebrates The Detroit Institute of Arts, located in Midtown Detroit, Michigan. The DIA has one of the largest art collections in the United States. With over 100 galleries, it covers 658,000 square feet with a major renovation and expansion project completed in 2007 that added 58,000 square feet.

SPECIAL ARTIST
This badge celebrates a special artist of the Scarab Club.

PRIDE MONTH
This pride month badge represents gay pride or LGBT pride. This month is the promotion of the self-affirmation, dignity, equality, and increased visibility of lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender people as a social group.
